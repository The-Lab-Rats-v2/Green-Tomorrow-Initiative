Our Programmes

Practical action for greener communities

Our programmes turn environmental awareness into hands-on projects that restore public spaces, reduce waste, and help communities build sustainable habits.

What we do

Featured Programmes

Each programme is designed to be accessible, community-led, and focused on visible environmental improvement.

Restoration

Community Tree Planting

We work with schools, residents, and local partners to plant trees, restore green spaces, and improve biodiversity in shared community areas.

Waste reduction

Recycling Campaigns

Our recycling campaigns help communities understand waste separation, responsible disposal, and simple ways to reduce single-use materials.

Clean energy

Renewable Energy Workshops

We host practical learning sessions that introduce clean energy options, energy-saving habits, and climate-conscious decision-making.
